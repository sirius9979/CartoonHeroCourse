About Kratos

"No. We will be the gods we choose to be, not those who have been. Who I was is not who you will be. We must be better."

Kratos, The God of War is a rage filled spartan imbued with the power of Ares. There are know four games in the God of War Uninverse and the most recently release shows Kratos off in a way that has never been done before. With completely new items, abilites, end-game progression, and of course the badass Blades of Chaos.
